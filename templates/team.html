Meet the PoPS Team

PoPS was started in 2018 in the Center for Geospatial Analytics at North Carolina State University in collaboration with USDA-APHIS. It has rapidly grown into a talented team of field scientists, computer scientists, and government analysts dedicated to changing the way the globe manages pests and pathogens.
